WebAbout

The Nike Air Zoom Pegasus is the epitome of comfort, speed, and innovation, built for runners who demand performance and style in every stride. Engineered with Nike’s signature Zoom Air units in the forefoot and heel, it delivers a springy, responsive ride that propels you forward, whether you're training for a marathon or chasing a personal best on your morning jog. The breathable mesh upper adapts to your foot for a snug, secure fit, while Flywire cables provide added support without sacrificing flexibility. A soft yet durable midsole foam cushions impact and reduces fatigue on long runs, making every mile feel easier. The outsole features a waffle-inspired pattern that enhances traction and durability across different terrains, allowing you to run with confidence in any weather. Lightweight and streamlined, the Pegasus doesn’t just perform—it looks good doing it, with bold Nike branding and sleek colorways that make a statement. Designed through decades of research and athlete feedback, it’s a shoe trusted by elite runners and everyday athletes alike. Whether you're sprinting on the track or cruising through city streets, the Nike Air Zoom Pegasus offers a smooth, energizing run that keeps you coming back for more. It’s more than just a shoe—it’s a running partner.
